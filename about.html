About Me

My home

Hi, my name is Matthew Lomas but I usually go by Matt. I am a born and breed Aussie from Brisbane, Queensland. I grew up in South-East Queensland until I was 22. That was when my partner and I decided that we were after a change of scenery and that we wanted to move to a more vibrant city. So in April 2019, we packed up our things and landed ourselves in Melbourne, Victoria.

Since 2014 I've worked in the Social Services sector, predominately in Aged Care and Siability Services. I've done some client case management, lead small teams, been through countless audits and see the face of social care evolve.

Most recently I've found work at a recruitment agency for Social Workers looking to make their next career move, I continue to lead a small team and maintin the compliance requirements of all our workers.

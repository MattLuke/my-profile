About Me

My home

So you want to get to know me a bit closer, eh? I get it, that home page didn't give you all the juicy details.
Well, you already know I'm Matt. I was born in raised in sunny Brisbane, Australia and lived there all my life until I was 22 years old when I decided to move to Melbourne, Australia.
I've been living here since 2019 and have been really enjoying the fresh breath of a new and vibrant city.

My education

When I graduated high school I decided that since I was really good at listening to everyone's problems that I would study psychology.I might as well have been getting paid for all the advice I was giving out. Plus, I didn't mind the idea of just sitting in a lounge chair all day listening to the woes of others and helping them find solutions.
Cut to when I actually started studying and it was not like what I had imagined at all! So, after a year, I decided that Psychology wasn't right for me and neither was on-campus study.
About a year later I remembered how I really enjoyed learning statistics in Psychology and how I'm not too shabby at Excel so started studying an online Bachelor in Accounting. That didn't last very long when I listened to my first lecture and wanted to bash my head into the table. Fastforward a few years to now, studying for a Bachelor in Information Technology and absolutely loving it. For the first time in a long time I'm enjoying learning again and I'm excited to see where it takes me.

My personality

According to Myers-Briggs

ISTP-T stands for Introvert, Sensing, Thinking, Perceiving and Turbulent.
Don't get me wrong, I love a party. Being predominately introverted for me means that after a lot of time around lots of people I need time alone, to recharge my social batteries. It also means that I am very careful at listening and won't always domineer a conversation. I also means I'm good at picking up social cues which make me good at reading situations.
